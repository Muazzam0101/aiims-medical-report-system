Our scoring logic is updated quarterly based on the latest medical guidelines from the WHO and local health authorities. The system utilizes a Bayesian inference model to account for missing data points, ensuring a score is generated even when partial records are available, though confidence intervals are adjusted accordingly.

What This System Does Not Do

It is critical to understand the boundaries of this clinical decision support tool.

  • warning

    Not a Diagnostic Device

    The system does not diagnose conditions. It identifies statistical patterns that may warrant further clinical investigation.

  • warning

    No Treatment Automation

    The system will never automatically order medications, procedures, or discharges without a licensed practitioner's signature.

  • warning

    Limited to Hospitalized Care

    Algorithms are currently optimized for acute inpatient settings and may produce inaccurate results for outpatient or chronic home care.
